Rules

Game objective

Guide Jack through the forest to recover all the stolen candy and defeat the enemies.
By completing the objective, Halloween will be saved and the world will be a happy place again.

Controls

Movements: Use the arrow keys to move Jack.
Jump: With the up arrow or the space key this action will be possible.
Objects: With the “a” key you can collect the objects you find.

Mechanics

Collection: Sweets are scattered throughout the world. Jack must collect them to win.
Evolution: By collecting enough candy to accumulate points.
Difficulty: the further you progress, the more difficult the game will be.
