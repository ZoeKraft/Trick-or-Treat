History

Main character

In a magical world where Halloween is in danger, a little pumpkin named Jack discovers that He has a destiny greater than he ever imagined. Some evil monsters have stolen all the candy from the kingdom to cause chaos. Without candy, the Halloween spirit will fade and the The world will fall into eternal darkness.
Jack, with his adventurous spirit, embarks on a great mission to recover the candy and save Halloween. Throughout his journey, he must go through eerie and dark forests, facing a wide variety of enemies along your path.

Enemies

These monsters came to end Halloween, they stole the candy and the spirit of this day, They will block the way so that Jack cannot save Halloween, you must avoid them to be able to get sweets and avoid losing.

Objects

The sweets will be a very important part, without them it is not possible to save Halloween, recover these Sweets will be the main mission of our pumpkin Jack, he must survive the attempt.

Goal

The goal of this game is not only to collect candy, but you must reach the bag of candy stolen by the enemies to win and be able to go to the next level!!

Scenery

In a dark and gloomy forest in the middle of the night, illuminated by the moonlight and lanterns The adventure of our pumpkin Jack will happen.
